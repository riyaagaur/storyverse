What Makes This Different

🎩 Sorting Ceremony

A conversational sorting by the Hat itself. Your house changes how every character treats you.

📖 Chapter Boundaries

Characters only know what's happened up to your selected chapter. No spoilers. No foreshadowing.

🤝 Trust Engine

Be kind and characters open up. Push too hard and they shut down. A 6-level emotional state that persists.

🎭 Multi-Character Scenes

Talk to Ron and Harry and Hermione are there — disagreeing, reacting, making the scene feel alive.

🛡️ Spoiler Resistance

Try to tell a character about the future. They'll be confused, skeptical — never accepting.

🔮 Private Context

Snape's love for Lily shapes every response. He will never tell you. That's the point.
